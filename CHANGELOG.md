## v4.1 — Problem Solving & Decision Modeling

A reusable **reasoning and decision layer** added on top of the existing system — explicitly scoped to not become another PRD template. Built by abstracting the reasoning patterns, causal chains, metric structures, hypothesis structures, decision rules, experimentation methods, rollout logic, and reusable formulas out of a single domain-specific source document, discarding every company-specific, product-specific, and domain-specific detail so the result is reusable across software, AI, robotics, hardware, SaaS, operations, logistics, manufacturing, product, growth, and strategy work.

- Added `skills/59-problem-solving-decision-modeling-skill/`: `SKILL.md`, four Modules (`174_Problem_Framing_OS.md`, `175_Causal_Metric_OS.md`, `176_Solution_Formula_OS.md`, `177_Validation_Rollout_OS.md`), and `FORMULA_LIBRARY.md` — 29 domain-neutral, reusable quantitative formula families (basic delta, weighted additive, fixed/variable cost, unit economics, revenue, funnel, cost-per-unit, profit/NPV, ROI, expected value, threshold rules, piecewise functions, multiplicative coefficients, capacity utilization, throughput/cycle-time/WIP, availability/reliability, quality/yield, experiment lift, weighted decision score, RICE, ICE, cost of delay/WSJF, sensitivity/elasticity, scenario modeling, confidence-adjusted impact, TCO, technical performance improvement, and composite objectives), each with equation, variable definitions, units, assumptions, valid/invalid use cases, and common mistakes. Copied the folder into `agents/CEO-Agent/59-problem-solving-decision-modeling-skill/` per the repo's existing convention of mirroring owned Skill folders into the owning Agent's directory.
- Assigned ownership to **CEO-Agent** (`agents/CEO-Agent/AGENT.md` updated to 5 Skills / 29 Modules) as a cross-cutting reasoning layer — the ninth Skill in that category alongside `14-validation-skill`, `15-framework-library-skill`, `16-artifact-template-skill`, `17-diagram-skill`, `25-prompt-loop-skill`, `26-knowledge-graph-skill`, `28-risk-management-skill`, and `40-meta-orchestration-skill` — since it activates alongside whichever domain Agent(s) a decision touches rather than owning a domain itself.
- Updated `registry/SKILL_REGISTRY.md` (new row, cross-cutting Skills list, total counts 58→59 Skills / 175→179 Modules) and `registry/AGENT_REGISTRY.md` (CEO-Agent's Skills table, Typical Outputs, total count).
- Updated `meta-agent/META_AGENT.md`: CEO-Agent's coverage row, the Agent Routing Rules table, a new "Cross-Cutting Skill Activation: Problem Solving and Decision Modeling" section describing how this Skill combines with domain Agents (robotics, SaaS pricing, AI infrastructure, manufacturing examples) instead of replacing them, and a new Multi-Agent Routing Examples row.
- Added `workflows/11-problem-solving-decision-workflow/WORKFLOW.md` — not a product-shaped workflow like `01`-`10`, but the reasoning workflow those ten call into at their own decision, prioritization, and validation gates; can also run standalone. Updated all 10 existing `workflows/*/WORKFLOW.md` files' Validation sections with a one-line cross-reference to this Skill at their respective major-decision points, rather than injecting it mechanically into every step.
- Added `commands/solve.md` (`/solve`) and regenerated `.claude/commands/` via `scripts/generate_claude_commands.py` (40 files, up from 39; verified the regeneration produced no diff on the other 39). Added a new "Reasoning & Decision Commands" table to `COMMANDS.md` and a note on how `/solve` composes with artifact commands (runs before them, not after).
- Extended `critic-agent/CRITIC_AGENT.md` (checks for misapplied formulas, double-counted benefits, correlation-as-causation, and unlabeled claims in a Decision Record), `planner-agent/PLANNER_AGENT.md` (carries a Staged Rollout Plan's stages and an Experiment Plan's stopping/rollback rules directly into the roadmap), and `reflection-agent/REFLECTION_AGENT.md` (backfills a Decision Record's Actual Outcome and Lesson fields, checks whether a hypothesis's falsification condition was ever tested).
- Extended `memory/decision-log.md` with a full **Decision Record** structure (Decision ID, Owner, Statement, Context, Options Considered, Selected Option, Evidence, Assumptions, Quantitative Model, Expected Outcome, Guardrails, Confidence, Review Date, Actual Outcome, Lesson) for decisions run through the new Skill, alongside the existing one-line decision-log format for everything else.
- Updated all 6 `knowledge-graph/*.md` files: `KNOWLEDGE_GRAPH.md` (Module/Skill/Workflow node counts), `SKILL_GRAPH.md` (new node, ninth cross-cutting Skill, dependency and graph-structure entries), `ARTIFACT_GRAPH.md` (new Decision Memo artifact family and its relationship notes), `WORKFLOW_GRAPH.md` (new `11-problem-solving-decision-workflow` node and relationships), `AGENT_GRAPH.md` (CEO-Agent's downstream note).

- Added `examples/decision-modeling-example.md`: three worked decisions (AI inference self-host vs. managed-API trade-off, a SaaS usage-based pricing tier, and a manufacturing inspection-automation call), each showing problem framing, causal/metric modeling, and formula selection without fabricated certainty. Indexed in `docs/EXAMPLES.md` and `README.md`'s Examples table.
