# Command
/cpo

## Purpose
Activates CPO-Agent for customer discovery, market sizing, product strategy, requirements, and the scorecards that prove product-market fit.

## Activated Agents
- CPO-Agent

## Activated Skills
- `01-discovery-skill` — problem statement, ICP, persona, JTBD
- `02-market-research-skill` — market sizing, competitor benchmarking
- `03-strategy-skill` — positioning, business model
- `04-prd-skill` — structured PRD
- `10-analytics-skill` — what to measure, dashboards
- `27-product-scorecard-skill` — PMF/retention/reliability scoring

## Workflows
- `01-new-product-workflow`

## Output
- Problem Statement
- ICP & Personas
- PRD
- Phased Roadmap
- North Star Metric & Scorecard

## Example
`/cpo Take this idea from problem statement to a structured PRD: [describe your product idea].`
