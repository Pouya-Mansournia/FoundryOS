# Command
/gtm

## Purpose
Generates a go-to-market plan — positioning, channels, pricing, and the campaign/retention motion that supports it.

## Activated Agents
- CRO-Agent
- CMO-Agent

## Activated Skills
- `08-gtm-skill` — channel strategy, funnel, GTM metrics
- `36-pricing-sales-skill` — pricing, packaging, sales motion
- `37-marketing-customer-success-skill` — demand gen, onboarding, retention

## Workflows
- `07-go-to-market-workflow`

## Output
- Positioning & Messaging
- Channel Strategy
- Pricing & Packaging
- Sales Process & Pipeline
- Campaign Calendar

## Example
`/gtm Build a go-to-market plan for launching our product in a new vertical.`
