# Command
/strategy

## Purpose
Builds or stress-tests strategy, positioning, and business model — at the product level (CPO-Agent) or the company level (CEO-Agent), depending on scope.

## Activated Agents
- CPO-Agent
- CEO-Agent *(conditional — company-level strategy)*

## Activated Skills
- `03-strategy-skill` — positioning, business model
- `02-market-research-skill` — competitive/market context
- `15-framework-library-skill` — strategy frameworks and anti-patterns
- `12-company-bible-skill` (CEO, conditional)

## Workflows
- `10-strategic-planning-workflow`

## Output
- Strategy & Positioning
- Business Model Canvas
- Competitor Analysis
- Strategic Roadmap

## Example
`/strategy Re-evaluate our positioning now that a new competitor has entered the market.`
