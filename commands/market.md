# Command
/market

## Purpose
Sizes the market and benchmarks the opportunity against competitors — narrower than `/strategy`, focused purely on the external landscape.

## Activated Agents
- CPO-Agent

## Activated Skills
- `02-market-research-skill` — TAM/SAM/SOM, competitor benchmarking

## Workflows
- `01-new-product-workflow`
- `10-strategic-planning-workflow`

## Output
- TAM/SAM/SOM
- Competitor Analysis
- Market Snapshot

## Example
`/market Size the opportunity for an edge-AI inspection product in industrial manufacturing.`
