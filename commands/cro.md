# Command
/cro

## Purpose
Activates CRO-Agent for go-to-market motion, channel and funnel design, pricing/packaging, and the sales pipeline that turns positioning into bookings.

## Activated Agents
- CRO-Agent

## Activated Skills
- `08-gtm-skill` — channel strategy, funnel, GTM metrics
- `36-pricing-sales-skill` — pricing, packaging, sales motion

## Workflows
- `07-go-to-market-workflow`

## Output
- Go-To-Market Plan
- Sales Funnel & Pipeline
- Revenue Strategy
- Channel Strategy & Pricing/Packaging

## Example
`/cro Build a go-to-market plan and sales pipeline for launching in a new customer segment.`
