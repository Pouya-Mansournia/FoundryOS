# 41 Mechatronics Skill

## What this skill does
Designs the mechanical, electronics, and embedded/firmware layers of a hardware product as one integrated mechatronics discipline -- tolerancing, FEA and DFM on the mechanical side; PCB, power, and signal integrity with EMC/regulatory compliance on the electronics side; and firmware, RTOS, and driver architecture on the embedded side -- feeding a single validation pass into the hardware NPI process.
